CHAS Clinics

About CHAS Clinics

The Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS) enables Singapore Citizens, including those from lower- to middle-income households, to receive subsidies for medical and dental care at participating General Practitioner (GP) and dental clinics.

Benefits of CHAS

  • 1. Subsidized medical and dental care at participating clinics.
  • 2. Comprehensive coverage for common illnesses, chronic conditions, and dental procedures.
  • 3. Accessibility to a wide network of clinics across Singapore.

Eligibility

Singapore Citizens of all ages are eligible for CHAS. The amount of subsidy is determined by household monthly income per person or the Annual Value (AV) of your home for households with no income.

How to Apply

Applications for CHAS can be submitted online through the Ministry of Health (MOH) website or by mailing a completed application form to the CHAS Processing Team.
